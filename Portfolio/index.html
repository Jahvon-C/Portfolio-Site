About Me

When I was young my parents always told me how playing video games too much was a bad idea but even so, I still continued to play more. The reason why I did was because I had already made made up my mind to become a Video Game Developer. That passion still runs strong in me as everytime I think about how I'm actually doing this it excites me! I studied Video Game Design and Development at TriOS College where we took a look at a bunch of different languages like: HTML5, C++, C#, Java, Swift, and Javascript. With what I learned during my time at TriOS I can share my passion through the projects I work on and live the dream!
